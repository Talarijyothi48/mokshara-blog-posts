🎯 Primary Target Audiences

Audience 1: The Pressured Gen X Caregiver

Age: 38–55

Profile: Working professional managing career, young children, AND ageing parents simultaneously. The "sandwich generation." Highly educated, digitally fluent, time-poor, guilt-rich.

Pain Points: Caregiver burnout, inability to find reliable home care information, family conflict about care decisions, guilt about not doing enough, own mental health declining

Search Behaviour: Informational ("how to talk to my parent about dementia"), late-night searches, mobile-dominant

Content Hook: "You are not failing. Here's what the research says you should do next."

Audience 2: The Senior Seeking Cognitive Resilience

Age: 60–78

Profile: Recently retired or approaching retirement, noticing cognitive changes, motivated to maintain independence. High health literacy. Has time and interest to read long-form content.

Pain Points: Fear of dementia/cognitive decline, isolation post-retirement, loss of purpose, health anxiety

Search Behaviour: Google-dominant, longer sessions, reads to completion, more likely to share with family

Content Hook: "Your brain is more adaptable than you've been told. Here's the science."

Audience 3: The Empathetic Adult Child (Gift Buyer)

Age: 30–45

Profile: Child or close relative of an elderly person. Not the primary caregiver but involved. Looking for resources, tools, or programs to help their parents and ease family stress.

Buying Motivation: eBooks, care planning guides, gift purchases for parents

Content Hook: "The most meaningful thing you can give your parents isn't a present — it's a plan."

Audience 4: The Wellness-Conscious Young Professional

Age: 25–38

Profile: Therapist, HR professional, yoga teacher, or wellness-aware millennial. Interested in mental health content for self-growth and professional development. Discovery via Instagram/LinkedIn.

Content Hook: "The neuroscience behind what actually reduces anxiety — vs. what just feels like it does."
